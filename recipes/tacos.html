Tacos!

Description

Chicken tacos with a salsa consisting of Pneapple, Tomato, Peppers,

Red onion and corriander. Serves 2

Ingredients:

  • 1kg Chicken Breast
  • Tortilla Shells
  • Tomatoes
  • Garlic
  • Red Onion
  • Peppers
  • Corriander
  • Avacados
  • Cheese

Steps:

  1. Prep chicken with seasoning and preheat oven to 180c
  2. Chop vegetables
  3. Get husband to pick corriander
  4. Mix vegetables in bowl
  5. Put chicken in the oven
  6. Fry tacos lightly
  7. Make guac
