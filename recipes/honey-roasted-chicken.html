Honey Roasted Chicken

Description:

Chicken roasted in honey. Serves 2.

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 Cup of Honey
  • 2 Tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 1/8 tbelspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1 1/2 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 2 SKinless, boneless chicken breast halves, cut into bite size pieces

Steps:

  1. Whisk honey, soy sauce nd pepper flakes in a bowl
  2. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat, add chicken till slightly browned
  3. Pour honey mixture in skillet, cook untill chicke is no longer pink in the middle
