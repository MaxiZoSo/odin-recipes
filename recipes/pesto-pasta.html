Pesto Pasta

Decription:

Pesto pasta in 15 mins. Serves 1.

Ingridients:

  • Pasta: Pick your favourite shape
  • Onion
  • Olive Oil
  • Pesto
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Parmesan Cheese

Steps:

  1. Boil water, add pasta and season with salt
  2. Cook pasta till al dente
  3. Fry onion in oil till translucent
  4. Add pesto to oil in pan and mix
  5. Drain pasta and add pesto mixture
  6. add cheese
  7. Serve!
